Welcome to Dota 2

Welcome to the exciting world of Dota 2, where battles are fierce and heroes are legendary.

Drow Ranger

Drow Ranger in Dota 2 is an agile ranged hero known for precision attacks and silencing enemies with Frost Arrows and Gust, while boosting allied ranged heroes' damage with Precision Aura.

Shadow Fiend

Shadow Fiend, a fearsome agility hero in Dota 2, wields dark powers to devastate foes with his razes and soul-stealing abilities, scaling in damage as he collects souls from fallen enemies.

Invoker

Invoker, a complex intelligence hero in Dota 2, manipulates three orbs to cast a diverse range of powerful spells, controlling battles with precision and adaptability.
